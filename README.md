# NEXUS-ECOM-PROJECT
A data analysis project based on Kaggle Data by Coby
Here is my project, based around the data from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/thedarkvenom/nexus-analytics-ecommerce-dataset.
I asked AI to create me 12 business questions based on this data, I cleaned and created the answers to my queries via BigQuery and ultimately completed my visualisation in Tableau. Below are my business questions as well as screenshots demonstrating my queries to answee them.
How much money did the business make over time? 
How many orders were placed over time? 
Which product categories perform the best? 
Which regions or locations buy the most? 
How does the average order value change over time? 
What are the top transactions by spending? 
Which subcategories bring in the least revenue? 
What days, weeks, or months are the busiest?
